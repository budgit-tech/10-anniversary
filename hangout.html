Not every time Budget analysis; sometimes go to the beach and relax….

At BudgIT, we play and we play hard. We know there’s no team-building without deliberate efforts towards ensuring everyone gets a chance to bond, unwind and get to know each other.

The Humans of BudgIT will show you what it means to relax, despite work demands.
‍
I mean, you should see what Damilola Ogundipe looks like with his sleeves rolled up at the beach. Seun Onigbinde and Gabriel Okeowo’s dance steps will have you wondering if all 30+  and 40+ men are actually really bad at dancing.

Can I say? Our PTOs are the real life of the party.
‍
These rare moments are usually fun, and we hope to catch lots of fun at our hangout.
‍
You can’t join, but we will send you pictures.

Our 10th anniversary (BudgIT @10) is a milestone, and we hope to tell our story and reel the public in  on our achievements in those years, as well as our goals and expectations for the next decade. 
Ride with us!
