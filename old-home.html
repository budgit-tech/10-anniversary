Celebrating a Decade of Civic Innovation and Impact

Over the last decade, BudgIT has recorded significant achievements while facilitating improved fiscal transparency and accountability through social advocacy, partnerships, institutional engagement and citizen engagement. We are not just telling our story, we are celebrating a decade of giant strides!

How it all started - a sneak peek

Get ready for a week of awesomeness! Whooop!

In the last ten years, BudgIT has facilitated budget reforms at both the national and sub-national levels through stakeholder and institutional engagements, collaborations, partnerships, civic-tech innovation and active citizen mobilisation. 
‍
Through our project tracking platform, Tracka, we have ensured effective service through project tracking and monitoring in 32 states. We have facilitated grassroots development and budget implementation in the grassroots through continuous institutional engagement and citizen mobilization.

So far we have ensured over 16,738 projects, tracked the completion of over 7000 constituency projects,  facilitated 15,000 town hall meetings through our 36 project tracking officers across Nigeria, with support from 1500 community champions currently present in 32 states.
‍
With over 200 million and 10 million digital and offline reaches in a decade, BudgIT is currently imprinting its footprints within Africa’s civic tech-space – Sierra Leone, Liberia, Ghana– and beyond.
‍
